Industry data

Written by Nathen Hogan and Gianni Capomolla

In this section we researched data supplied by Burning Glass and other sources which were sought out. This Information was then used to analyse the ideal jobs chosen by each member of the team. This report will cover the IT-specific skills and how the rank in terms of demand from employers regarding these skillsets, it will also report on the general (non-IT) skills required and their rank of demand form employers. Further it will explain the highest ranked skills, both IT and general, which are not required of the team's skillsets.

The layout which content will be presented will display as follows; Job title and description for each member; the IT skills required for each member; the general skills required for each member; Highest ranked IT skills not in skillsets for each member; Highest ranked general skills not in skillset for each member; Following this data, each member was asked if their ideal job had changed at all and each member will provide their answer in the end section of report.
